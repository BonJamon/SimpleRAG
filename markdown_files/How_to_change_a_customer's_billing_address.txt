Changing a Customer's Billing Address
How to Change a Customer's Billing Address
Important: Ensure you have verified the customer's identity and have appropriate authorization to make changes to their billing information.
Please follow the steps below to change a customer's billing address:
Log in to the customer account using your administrative credentials.
Navigate to the "Billing" section on the customer dashboard.
Click on "Edit Billing Address."
Enter the new billing address details in the provided fields.
Double-check the entered information for accuracy.
Click "Save Changes" to update the billing address.
Confirm the change with the customer and inform them they will receive an email confirmation.
Field
Description
Street Address
The customer's new street address.
City
The city of the new billing address.
State/Province
The state or province of the new billing address.
Zip/Postal Code
The zip or postal code for the new billing address.
Country
The country of the new billing address.
