Activating a New Modem or Router
Welcome to the QuickConnect guide on how to assist customers in activating their new modem or router. Follow the steps below to ensure a smooth activation process.
Steps to Activate a New Modem or Router
Unbox and Connect the Device
Ensure all components are present (modem/router, power adapter, cables).
Connect the modem/router to a power source.
Use the provided Ethernet cable to connect the modem/router to the computer.
If activating a modem, connect the coaxial cable from the wall jack to the modem's coaxial port.
Power Up and Wait for Initialization
Turn on the modem/router and wait for it to go through its initialization process. This may take a few minutes. Look for indicator lights to turn solid or stop flashing.
Access the Activation Page
Open a web browser on the connected computer.
Visit the QuickConnect activation page: http://activate.quickconnect.com.
Follow On-Screen Instructions
Follow the instructions provided on the activation page. You may need the following information:
Account number
Billing ZIP code
MAC address of the modem/router (usually found on the device label)
Verify Internet Connection
Once activation is complete, the modem/router will restart.
After the restart, check if the internet connection is active by visiting a website.
Contact QuickConnect Customer Support if Needed
If the customer faces any issues, advise them to contact QuickConnect customer support for further assistance.
Troubleshooting Tips
Problem
Solution
Modem/Router is not powering on
Check the power connection and ensure the power outlet is functional.
No internet connection after activation
Restart the modem/router and the computer. Check all cable connections.
Unable to access the activation page
Ensure the computer is connected to the modem/router via Ethernet cable. Try a different web browser.
Note: For any persistent issues, customers should contact QuickConnect customer support at 1-800-123-4567.
