Troubleshooting Steps
Verify Email Settings: Ensure that the email client settings are correct.
Check Connectivity: Confirm that the internet connection is stable and secure.
Review Email Queues: Look for emails stuck in the outbox or drafts.
Contact Support: If the issue persists, contact the email service provider for further assistance.
Note: Always make sure that customers are using the latest version of their email client and that it is properly configured to avoid any known bugs or issues.
