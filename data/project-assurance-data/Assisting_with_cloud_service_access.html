Assisting with Cloud Service Access

This document is designed to help QuickConnect customer service agents assist customers with accessing and troubleshooting our cloud services.

Steps to Assist with Cloud Service Access

  1. Verify Customer Identity: Confirm the customer's identity through security questions.
  2. Confirm Service Subscription: Ensure the customer has a valid subscription to the cloud service they are trying to access.
  3. Guide Through Login Procedure: Provide assistance on how to log in to the cloud service using the appropriate credentials.
  4. Troubleshoot Common Issues: Help the customer troubleshoot common access issues.

Common Issues and Solutions

Issue Solution
Forgot Password Guide the customer through the password reset process available on the login page.
Account Locked Inform the customer on the waiting period for account unlock or guide them on how to contact support for further assistance.
Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) Issues Assist in verifying 2FA settings or guide the customer on how to use backup codes to access their account.
Service Outage Check for any reported outages. Inform the customer and provide an estimated resolution time if available.
