Guidelines for Upgrading Network Equipment

QuickConnect is dedicated to ensuring that our customers have the best possible experience with their network equipment. This document provides a comprehensive set of guidelines for customer service agents to assist customers with upgrading their network equipment.

1. Initial Assessment

  • Confirm the customer's current equipment setup. This includes routers, modems, extenders, and switches.
  • Determine the reasons for the upgrade, which may include:
    • Improved performance
    • Extended coverage
    • Increased security
    • Support for more devices
  • Check the compatibility of new equipment with the customer's existing setup.
  • Assess the customer's internet plan to ensure it supports the new equipment capabilities.

3. Step-by-Step Upgrade Guide

Preparing for the Upgrade

  • Back up current network settings.
  • Ensure you have all necessary equipment and cables.
  • Read the user manuals of the new devices beforehand.

Installing New Equipment

  • Disconnect old equipment and unplug all cables.
  • Connect the new modem to the cable outlet and power source.
  • Attach the router to the modem using an Ethernet cable.
  • Power on the modem first, wait for it to fully initialize, then power on the router.
