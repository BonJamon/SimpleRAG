Setting up Automatic Bill Payments

Automatic bill payments provide convenience and ensure that customers' payments are always made on time. Here’s how to guide a customer through setting up automatic bill payments with QuickConnect.

Steps to Set Up Automatic Bill Payments:

  1. Log in to the QuickConnect customer portal at https://www.quickconnect.com/portal.
  2. Navigate to the "Billing" section from the main menu.
  3. Select "Payment Methods" from the dropdown menu.
  4. Under "Automatic Payments", click on "Set Up Automatic Payments".
  5. Enter the required billing information including bank account or credit card details.
  6. Review the terms and conditions and check the box to agree.
  7. Click on "Submit" to complete the setup process.

Note: Customers should ensure that their payment method is up to date to avoid any disruptions in the automatic billing process.

Common Issues and Solutions:

Issue Solution
Payment Method Declined Verify that the payment information is correct and that there are sufficient funds or credit available.
Unable to Log In Ensure that the customer is using the correct username and password. If needed, guide them through the password reset process.
Automatic Payment Not Processing Check if there are any holds or issues on the customer’s account. Ensure that their payment method details are up to date.
