Adjust Parental Control Settings

Adjusting parental control settings helps manage the content accessible on your network. Follow these steps to ensure a safe and secure browsing experience for your family:

Steps to Adjust Parental Control Settings:

Step 1: Log in to the Router's Web Interface
  • Open a web browser and type 192.168.1.1 or 192.168.0.1 in the address bar.
  • Enter your router's username and password (default: admin/admin or admin/password).
Step 2: Navigate to the Parental Control Section
  • After logging in, locate the Parental Control section, usually found under "Security" or "Advanced Settings".
  • Click on "Parental Controls" to access the settings.
Step 3: Enable Parental Controls
  • Toggle the Parental Control switch to ON.
  • Set the primary restrictive settings such as blocking specific websites and creating time schedules.
Step 4: Customize Content Filters
  • Under the content filter section, add keyword filters or websites you wish to block.
  • You may also allow specific sites if your service supports exceptions.
Step 5: Set Time Restrictions
  • Navigate to the "Time Schedule" option within the Parental Control menu.
  • Set the permissible internet access hours for different devices connected to the network.
Step 6: Save Settings and Apply Changes
  • After customizing your preferences, click on Save or Apply to implement your settings.
Note: The interface and options may vary slightly depending on the make and model of your router.
