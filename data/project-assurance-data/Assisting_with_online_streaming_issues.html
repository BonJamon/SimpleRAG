Assisting Customers with Online Streaming Issues

Guiding customers through resolving online streaming issues, including buffering, poor video quality, and connectivity problems, to ensure a seamless experience.

Issue Possible Causes Solution
Buffering 1. Slow internet speed
2. Network congestion
  1. Run a speed test and ensure minimum required speed.
  2. Reduce the number of devices connected to the network.
  3. Restart the router/modem.
Poor Video Quality 1. Low bandwidth
2. Streaming settings
  1. Check and upgrade the internet plan if needed.
  2. Change the streaming quality settings to a lower resolution if bandwidth is an issue.
Connectivity Issues 1. Network configuration
2. Outdated firmware
