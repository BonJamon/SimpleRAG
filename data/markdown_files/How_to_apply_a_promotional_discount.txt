QuickConnect Customer Service: Applying a Promotional Discount
How to Apply a Promotional Discount
This document provides detailed steps for QuickConnect customer service agents to assist customers in applying promotional discounts to their accounts. Follow these procedures to ensure a smooth and efficient process.
Steps to Apply a Promotional Discount
Step
Description
1. Access the Customer Account
Log in to the customer management system.
Search for the customer's account using their account number or phone number.
2. Validate the Promotional Offer
Confirm the customer is eligible for the promotional discount.
Check the promotion's terms and conditions.
3. Apply the Discount
Navigate to the promotions section within the customer's account.
Select the appropriate promotional discount from the list.
Enter the promotional code if required.
Confirm the discount is applied by reviewing the customerâ€™s billing summary.
4. Confirm with the Customer
Inform the customer that the discount has been successfully applied.
Provide the updated billing details and next billing date.
Important Points to Remember
Always verify the terms and conditions of the promotional discount.
Inform the customer if the promotion is a one-time discount or a recurring discount.
If there are any issues applying the discount, escalate it to the team leader or appropriate department.
Keep a record of all promotions applied to assist with any future customer inquiries.
