Setting up Automatic Bill Payments
Automatic bill payments provide convenience and ensure that customers' payments are always made on time. Here’s how to guide a customer through setting up automatic bill payments with QuickConnect.
Steps to Set Up Automatic Bill Payments:

Log in to the QuickConnect customer portal at https://www.quickconnect.com/portal.
Navigate to the "Billing" section from the main menu.
Select "Payment Methods" from the dropdown menu.
Under "Automatic Payments", click on "Set Up Automatic Payments".
Enter the required billing information including bank account or credit card details.
Review the terms and conditions and check the box to agree.
Click on "Submit" to complete the setup process.


Note: Customers should ensure that their payment method is up to date to avoid any disruptions in the automatic billing process.
