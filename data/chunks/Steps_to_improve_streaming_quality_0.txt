Steps to Improve Streaming Quality
Streaming quality can be affected by various factors, including network issues, device performance, and configuration settings. Below are detailed steps and solutions to help enhance streaming quality for QuickConnect customers.
General Tips for Enhancing Streaming Quality

Ensure you have a stable internet connection with adequate bandwidth.
Reduce the number of devices connected to your network during streaming.
Close any applications running in the background that may consume bandwidth.
Consider using an Ethernet cable instead of WiFi to reduce lag and improve stability.
Restart your router and streaming device regularly to clear any temporary issues.
