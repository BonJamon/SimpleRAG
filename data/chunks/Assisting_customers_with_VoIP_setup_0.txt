QuickConnect Customer Service: VoIP Setup Guide
Welcome to the comprehensive guide on assisting customers with VoIP (Voice over Internet Protocol) setup. This document will provide detailed steps and tips to help you support our customers efficiently.
1. Pre-Setup Checklist

Ensure the customer has a stable internet connection.
Verify the customer has a compatible VoIP phone or adapter.
Have the customer's QuickConnect account information ready.
Ensure that the customer's router and modem are functioning correctly.
Check if the necessary cables and connectors are available.

2. Setting Up VoIP Hardware
2.1 Connecting the VoIP Phone

Plug the Ethernet cable into the Ethernet port on the VoIP phone.
Connect the other end of the Ethernet cable to an available LAN port on the customer's router.
Connect the power adapter to the VoIP phone and plug it into a power outlet.
Wait for the VoIP phone to initialize and display readiness for configuration.

2.2 Using a VoIP Adapter

Connect one end of the Ethernet cable to the WAN port of the VoIP adapter.
Connect the other end of the Ethernet cable to an available LAN port on the customer's router.
Plug the VoIP phone into the VoIP adapter using a telephone cable.
Connect the power adapter to the VoIP adapter and plug it into a power outlet.
Wait for the VoIP adapter to initialize and display readiness for configuration.

3. Configuring VoIP Settings
3.1 Accessing the VoIP Configuration Interface

Open a web browser and enter the IP address of the VoIP phone or adapter. This information is usually provided in the device's manual.
Log in using the default username and password. Common defaults are often "admin" for both fields.

3.2 Entering SIP Account Details

Navigate to the SIP settings section of the configuration interface.
Enter the following details provided by QuickConnect:

            
SIP Server: sip.quickconnect.com
SIP Username: [Customer's QuickConnect Username]
SIP Password: [Customer's QuickConnect Password]
Outbound Proxy: proxy.quickconnect.com (if applicable)


Save the changes and restart the device if prompted.
