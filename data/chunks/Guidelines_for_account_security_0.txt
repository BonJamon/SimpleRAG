QuickConnect Customer Support: Account Security Guidelines




Guidelines for Account Security
Ensuring the security of our customers' accounts is paramount. This document provides guidelines for maintaining and supporting account security. Please follow these procedures when assisting customers.
Best Practices for Customers

Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
Change passwords regularly.
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
Regularly monitor account activity for any unauthorized access.
Be cautious of phishing attempts and suspicious emails.

Steps for Securing an Account


Step
Description


1. Verify Identity
Ask the customer to provide their account number, date of birth, and a recent transaction for verification.


2. Reset Password
Guide the customer through the process of resetting their password.


3. Enable 2FA
Assist the customer in enabling two-factor authentication for an added layer of security.


4. Review Account Activity
Suggest the customer to review recent account activities and report any suspicious actions immediately.


5. Update Security Questions
Encourage the customer to update their security questions and answers for additional protection.


Customer Support Protocol

Maintain confidentiality of customer information at all times.
Provide clear, step-by-step instructions when guiding customers through security processes.
Verify identity before making any changes to an account.
Log all interactions and actions taken during the support session.


Note: Always be vigilant and report any suspicious activity or anomalies to the security team immediately.
