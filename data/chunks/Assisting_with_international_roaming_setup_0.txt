QuickConnect Customer Service Guide: International Roaming Setup



QuickConnect Customer Service Guide
Assisting with International Roaming Setup
Overview
When customers travel abroad, they may want to use their mobile devices to make calls, send texts, and access the internet. Assisting customers with setting up international roaming is a common query. This guide provides steps to support customers with this process.
Steps for Setting Up International Roaming

Verify that the customer's account is eligible for international roaming.
Ensure the customer understands the potential costs associated with international roaming.
Guide the customer through enabling international roaming on their device.
Confirm the device settings to ensure connectivity while abroad.

1. Verify Account Eligibility
Check the following:

The customer's plan includes international roaming features.
The customer has not exceeded any usage limits that would restrict roaming.

2. Cost Awareness
Make sure the customer is aware of:

Additional fees: Inform the customer about potential additional charges for calls, texts, and data usage while roaming.
Available packages: Suggest international packages that might help reduce costs.
Billing cycle: Explain how the customer will be billed for their roaming usage.


Tip: Direct customers to the QuickConnect website or app where they can view and manage their international roaming options.

3. Enabling International Roaming on the Device
Guide the customer with the steps specific to their device type:


Device
Steps


iPhone


Open Settings.
Tap Cellular (or Mobile Data).
Tap Cellular Data Options.
Toggle on Data Roaming.




Android


Open Settings.
Tap Connections.
Tap Mobile Networks.
Toggle on Data Roaming.




4. Confirming Device Settings
Ensure the following settings are properly configured:

Network Selection: Set to automatic to allow the device to connect to available networks abroad.
