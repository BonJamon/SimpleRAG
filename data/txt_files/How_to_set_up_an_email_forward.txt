




QuickConnect Customer Support - Setting Up Email Forwarding




How to Set Up Email Forwarding
This document provides a step-by-step guide to assist QuickConnect customers in setting up email forwarding. Follow these instructions to ensure that all your emails are forwarded to another email address of your choice.
Step-by-Step Instructions


Log into your QuickConnect Email Account

Open your web browser and go to the QuickConnect email login page.
Enter your email address and password, then click "Sign In."



Navigate to Settings

Once logged in, locate and click on the "Settings" gear icon, usually found at the top-right corner of the webpage.
In the drop-down menu, select "See All Settings."



Access the Forwarding and POP/IMAP Tab

In the settings menu, click on the "Forwarding and POP/IMAP" tab.



Add a Forwarding Address

Click the "Add a forwarding address" button.
Enter the email address you want to forward your emails to in the dialog box that appears, and then click "Next."
A verification email will be sent to the specified email address; click "Proceed" to initiate this.



Verify the Forwarding Address

Log into the email account where you want to receive the forwarded emails.
Open the verification email from QuickConnect and click on the verification link provided within.
Return to your QuickConnect email settings where you initially added the forwarding address.



Enable Email Forwarding

After verifying, go to the "Forwarding and POP/IMAP" tab again.
Select the "Forward a copy of incoming mail to" option and choose the newly added email address.
Determine what you want QuickConnect to do with the original emails (e.g., keep QuickConnect's copy in the Inbox, archive, or delete it).
Click "Save Changes" at the bottom of the settings page to apply the changes.



Troubleshooting
If you encounter issues while setting up email forwarding, consider the following tips:

Ensure you have access to the forwarding email address to complete the verification process.
Double-check that you entered the forwarding email address correctly.
Make sure there are no filters in place that might affect email forwarding.
Contact QuickConnect customer support if you continue to have issues or need further assistance.
