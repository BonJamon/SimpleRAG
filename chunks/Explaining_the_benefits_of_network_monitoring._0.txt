Enhanced Security
Continuous network monitoring helps in identifying and mitigating security threats in real-time, protecting sensitive data and preventing unauthorized access.

Network monitoring tools can detect potential issues before they become critical, allowing for proactive maintenance and minimizing downtime.

Monitoring ensures that network performance is optimized by identifying bottlenecks and enabling timely interventions to address them.

By identifying and resolving issues quickly, network monitoring helps in maintaining high availability and reliability of services.
