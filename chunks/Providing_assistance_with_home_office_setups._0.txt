Tips for an Effective Home Office Setup
Upgrade Equipment: Modern routers and modems can provide better speed and reliability. Recommend equipment that supports the latest Wi-Fi standards.
Positioning: Place the router in a central location, away from obstructions and other electronic devices that might cause interference.
Network Extenders: For larger homes, suggest Wi-Fi extenders or mesh networks to improve coverage.
Device Management: Advise customers to limit the number of connected devices or allocate bandwidth to critical devices.
Regular Updates: Ensure firmware for all devices is up to date to benefit from the latest security patches and performance improvements.
