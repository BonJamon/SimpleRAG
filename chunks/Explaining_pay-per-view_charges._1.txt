Assisting with Parental Controls
Guide the customer to the "Settings" section of their QuickConnect account.
Select "Parental Controls" from the options available.
Instruct them to set a pin code or password to restrict PPV purchases.
Inform them how to disable the controls if needed, using the same settings menu.
