Open a web browser and enter the modem's IP address (e.g., 192.168.1.1).
Login with the modem's admin credentials.
Verify that settings such as Network Mode and Channel are configured correctly.


Update Modem Firmware:

Navigate to the firmware section of the modem settings.
Check for any available firmware updates and install them.
Restart the modem after the update is complete.


Check for Signal Interference:

Move the modem away from potential sources of interference (e.g., microwaves, cordless phones).
Ensure the modem is placed in a central, elevated location.


Contact ISP Support:

If the issue persists, contact the ISP to check for any outages or issues on their end.
