QuickConnect: Setting Up and Managing Family Sharing




How to Set Up and Manage Family Sharing
Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Create a Family Group

Log in to your QuickConnect account on the website or mobile app.
Go to the 'Family Sharing' section under 'Account Settings.'
Click on 'Create Family Group.'
Follow the prompts to name your family group and add an optional family photo.



Step 2: Invite Family Members

In the 'Family Sharing' section, select 'Add Family Member.'
Enter the email addresses or phone numbers of the family members you want to invite.
Add a personalized message if desired.
Send the invitations. Your family members will receive a notification to join your Family Group.



Step 3: Manage Shared Content

Go to the 'Shared Content' section under 'Family Sharing.'
Select the types of content you want to share with your family group (e.g., data plans, app subscriptions).
Adjust permissions as necessary for each family member.


Frequently Asked Questions

Can I remove a family member from the group?
Yes, as the Family Organizer, you can remove members. Go to 'Family Sharing,' select the member you want to remove, and click on 'Remove from Family Group.'


Is there a limit to the number of family members?
You can add up to 6 members (including yourself) to your Family Group.
