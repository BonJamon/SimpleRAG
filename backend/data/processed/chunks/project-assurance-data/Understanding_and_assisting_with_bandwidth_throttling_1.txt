Steps to Assist Customers



Step
Action




Step 1
Verify the customer’s current plan and check for any data cap limitations.


Step 2
Check for network outages or maintenance work in the customer's area.


Step 3
Advise the customer to run a speed test and compare the results with their plan speeds.


Step 4
Instruct the customer to reduce bandwidth-heavy activities during peak hours.


Step 5
If throttling is confirmed and significant, discuss upgrading the plan or contacting higher-level support.



Additional Tips

Encourage regular monitoring of data usage to avoid unexpected throttling.
Inform customers about peak hours and suggest off-peak usage for heavy internet activities.
Consider providing information on QuickConnect’s higher bandwidth plans and any special offers or promotions.
