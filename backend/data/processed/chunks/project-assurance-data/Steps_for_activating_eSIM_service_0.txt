Steps for Activating eSIM Service
Introduction
Activating your eSIM service with QuickConnect is a straightforward process. This document will guide you through each step to ensure you have a seamless experience. Please follow the steps below carefully.
Requirements

An eSIM-compatible device.
Access to a stable internet connection.
Your QuickConnect account credentials.
An active eSIM QR code provided by QuickConnect.

Activation Steps



Step
Description




Step 1
Go to your device’s settings and select "Cellular" or "Mobile".


Step 2
Choose "Add Cellular Plan".


Step 3
Scan the provided eSIM QR code using your device’s camera.


Step 4
Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the setup.


Step 5
Once added, make sure to set the eSIM as your primary line or secondary line according to your needs.




Note: Ensure that your device is updated to the latest software version to avoid any compatibility issues.

        
Troubleshooting

If the eSIM QR code is not scanning, try cleaning your camera lens or increasing the brightness of your screen.
If you encounter any errors during the setup, restart your device and attempt the process again.
Ensure that you have a stable internet connection throughout the activation process.
