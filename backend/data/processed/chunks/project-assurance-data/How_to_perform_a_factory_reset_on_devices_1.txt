Smartphone


Navigate to the Settings menu on your smartphone.
Select "Backup & reset" or "System", then choose "Factory data reset".
You may be prompted to enter your passcode or unlock pattern.
Confirm by selecting "Reset" or "Erase everything".
Wait for the phone to reset and restart. This may take several minutes.




Tablet


Open the Settings menu on your tablet.
Choose "System" and then "Reset options".
Select "Erase all data (factory reset)".
Confirm your selection and follow the on-screen prompts to initiate the reset.
Allow the tablet to reset and restart. This process can take a few minutes.




Important Notes

Backing up your data is recommended before performing a factory reset, as this process will erase all personal data and settings.
If you encounter any issues during the reset process, contact QuickConnect Customer Support for assistance.
After a factory reset, you may need to reconfigure your device settings and reconnect to your Wi-Fi network.
