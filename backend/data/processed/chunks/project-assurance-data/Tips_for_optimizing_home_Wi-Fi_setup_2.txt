Common Troubleshooting Steps
Even with an optimal setup, Wi-Fi issues can still occur. Here are some common troubleshooting steps:



Issue
Solution




Slow Connection
Restart the router, check for interference, limit the number of devices, or switch to a less crowded channel.


Connection Drops
Update firmware, reduce interference, or consider using a wired connection for critical devices.


Devices Not Connecting
Ensure the correct password is entered, forget the network and reconnect, or reset the network settings on the device.



By following these tips and guidelines, customers can enhance their home Wi-Fi experience
