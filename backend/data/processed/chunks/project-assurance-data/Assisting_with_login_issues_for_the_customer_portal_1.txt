Important Tips

Always verify the customer's identity before making any changes to their account.
Document all actions taken in the customer's support ticket for future reference.
Never request or record the customer's password for security reasons.

Contacting Technical Support
If you've followed all troubleshooting steps and the customer's login issue remains unresolved, escalate the case to technical support. Provide them with the customer's details and a summary of the steps you've taken so far.
