3. Enabling International Roaming on the Device
Guide the customer with the steps specific to their device type:


Device
Steps


iPhone


Open Settings.
Tap Cellular (or Mobile Data).
Tap Cellular Data Options.
Toggle on Data Roaming.




Android


Open Settings.
Tap Connections.
Tap Mobile Networks.
Toggle on Data Roaming.




4. Confirming Device Settings
Ensure the following settings are properly configured:

Network Selection: Set to automatic to allow the device to connect to available networks abroad.
