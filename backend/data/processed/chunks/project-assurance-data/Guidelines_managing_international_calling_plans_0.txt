Guidelines for Managing International Calling Plans
Welcome to QuickConnect's guide on managing international calling plans. This document will help you provide excellent customer support by understanding the key aspects of international calling plans, how to modify them, and ensure customers are on the best plan for their needs.

Plan Restrictions

Ensure that the plan covers the countries the customer intends to call frequently. Coverage can vary between plans.


Billing Cycle
Inform the customer about when the billing cycle starts and ends, and how international calls are billed.
