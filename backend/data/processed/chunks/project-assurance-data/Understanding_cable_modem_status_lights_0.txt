Understanding Cable Modem Status Lights
Customer service agents at QuickConnect may often receive queries regarding the status lights on cable modems. These lights provide vital information about the modem's performance and connection status. Understanding what each light indicates can help resolve issues more swiftly and effectively.
Common Status Lights on Cable Modems



Status Light
Indicator Color
Description
Troubleshooting Tips




Power
Green
The modem is powered on and functioning properly.
Ensure the power cable is securely connected.


Downstream
Green/Amber
Green: Modem is receiving a strong signal. Amber: Searching for downstream frequency.
Check cable connections and ensure there are no service outages.


Upstream
Green/Amber
Green: Modem is transmitting data. Amber: Searching for upstream frequency.
Check cable connections and ensure there are no service outages.


Online
Green
The modem is online and connected to the internet.
If not lit, try rebooting the modem or checking connections.


LAN/WAN
Green
Indicates active connection to a device (e.g., router, computer).
Ensure the Ethernet cable is securely connected and the device is powered on.
