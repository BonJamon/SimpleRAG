Resolving Network Login Issues
Welcome to the QuickConnect support documentation. This guide provides a comprehensive approach for customer service agents to resolve network login issues faced by customers.
Common Network Login Issues
Customers may face a variety of network login issues. Here are the most common types:

Incorrect Username or Password
Account Locked
Connectivity Problems
Expired Passwords

Troubleshooting Steps
Step 1: Verify Customer Information
Ensure that you have the correct username and password from the customer. Verify their identity by following company security protocols.

Step 3: Unlock Account
If the customer's account is locked, guide them through the unlock process:

Inform the customer of the lockout reason (e.g., multiple failed login attempts).
Initiate the unlock procedure by verifying security questions or OTP.
Reset the password if necessary and advise the customer to use the new credentials.

Step 4: Connectivity Checks
Ensure that the customer has a stable internet connection. Ask them to perform the following:

Restart their device.
Check Wi-Fi router settings or network cables.
Attempt to login using a different device or browser.
