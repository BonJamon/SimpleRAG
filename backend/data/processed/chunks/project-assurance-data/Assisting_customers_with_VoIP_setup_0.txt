QuickConnect Customer Service: VoIP Setup Guide
Welcome to the comprehensive guide on assisting customers with VoIP (Voice over Internet Protocol) setup. This document will provide detailed steps and tips to help you support our customers efficiently.
1. Pre-Setup Checklist

Ensure the customer has a stable internet connection.
Verify the customer has a compatible VoIP phone or adapter.
Have the customer's QuickConnect account information ready.
Ensure that the customer's router and modem are functioning correctly.
Check if the necessary cables and connectors are available.

2. Setting Up VoIP Hardware
2.1 Connecting the VoIP Phone

Plug the Ethernet cable into the Ethernet port on the VoIP phone.
Connect the other end of the Ethernet cable to an available LAN port on the customer's router.
Connect the power adapter to the VoIP phone and plug it into a power outlet.
Wait for the VoIP phone to initialize and display readiness for configuration.
