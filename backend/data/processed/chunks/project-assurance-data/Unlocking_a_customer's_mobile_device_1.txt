Inform the Customer

                
Let the customer know that the unlock request has been submitted.
Provide an estimated time-frame for processing (usually within 2-3 business days).



After Unlock Request is Processed

The customer will receive an email confirmation once the device is successfully unlocked.
Instruct the customer to follow the steps in the email to complete the unlocking process on their device.

Troubleshooting Common Issues



Issue
Solution




Unlock request denied
Verify the device's eligibility criteria. Ensure all pending bills are cleared and the device isn't reported lost or stolen.


Customer didn't receive email confirmation
Ask the customer to check their spam/junk folder. Resubmit the unlock request if necessary.


Device failed to unlock
Check if the correct IMEI number was entered. Advise the customer to follow the instructions in the email carefully.



Contact for Further Support
If the customer encounters any further issues, direct them to our technical support
