Free Local Resources
Customers can also take advantage of free local resources to improve their digital literacy:

Public Libraries: Many libraries offer free computer classes and internet access.
Community Centers: Look for workshops and courses at community centers in your area.
Non-Profit Organizations: Some non-profits provide digital literacy programs for different age groups.
