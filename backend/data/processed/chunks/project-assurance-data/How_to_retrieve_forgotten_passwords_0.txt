QuickConnect Customer Support: Retrieve Forgotten Passwords




QuickConnect Customer Support
How to Retrieve Forgotten Passwords
If a customer has forgotten their password, follow the steps below to assist them in retrieving or resetting their password:


Step
Action
Details


1
Go to the Login Page
Direct the customer to the QuickConnect login page at quickconnect.com/login.


2
Click on "Forgot Password"
Have the customer click on the "Forgot Password" link located below the login form.


3
Enter Email Address
Ask the customer to enter their registered email address in the designated field.


4
Check Email Inbox
Instruct the customer to check their email inbox for a password reset link. They should also check their spam/junk folder.


5
Click on Reset Link
Have the customer click on the password reset link received in their email.


6
Enter New Password
Direct the customer to enter a new password and confirm it on the reset password page.


7
Login with New Password
Ask the customer to return to the login page and log in using their email and new password.
