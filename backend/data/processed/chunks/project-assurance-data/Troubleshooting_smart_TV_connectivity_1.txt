2. Frequent Disconnections
If the Smart TV frequently disconnects from the network, try the following:

Change the Wi-Fi channel on the router to a less crowded one.
Check for any available firmware updates for the router and the Smart TV.
Consider using a wired Ethernet connection for a more stable connection.

3. Poor Signal Strength
Improve the signal strength with these steps:

Reposition the router to a central location.
Avoid placing the router near thick walls or metal objects.
Use a Wi-Fi range extender to boost the signal in the TV's location.
Check for any physical damage to the router or TV's Wi-Fi antenna.
