Steps for Configuring Email Clients
Follow the steps below to configure your email client with QuickConnect services. Make sure you have your account information handy, including your email address and password.
1. Microsoft Outlook


Step
Details


1
Open Outlook and go to File > Add Account.


2
Enter your email address and click Connect.


3
Choose IMAP or POP for the account type.


4
Enter the incoming and outgoing server details:

            
Incoming Mail Server: imap.quickconnect.com
Outgoing Mail Server: smtp.quickconnect.com




5
Enter your account credentials (email address and password).


6
Click Next and then Finish.


2. Apple Mail


Step
Details


1
Open Apple Mail and go to Mail > Add Account.


2
Select Other Mail Account and click Continue.


3
Enter your email address and password, then click Sign In.


4
Fill in the server information:

            
Incoming Mail Server: imap.quickconnect.com
Outgoing Mail Server: smtp.quickconnect.com




5
Click Sign In and then click Done.
