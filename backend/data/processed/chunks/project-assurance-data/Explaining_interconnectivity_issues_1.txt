Slow internet speeds
Congestion, interference, or incorrect settings
Run a speed test, move closer to the router, and optimize settings


Unable to connect to VPN
VPN configuration issues or ISP restrictions
Verify VPN settings and check with ISP for any restrictions


Intermittent connectivity
Wireless interference or router firmware issue
Change wireless channels and update router firmware





Escalation Procedure
If the issue cannot be resolved through basic troubleshooting, follow these escalation steps:

Document all troubleshooting steps taken and results observed.
Collect key information such as customer account number, device model, and network setup.
Create a detailed support ticket and escalate it to the Level 2 support team.
Inform the customer of expected response times and provide any interim solutions if possible.
