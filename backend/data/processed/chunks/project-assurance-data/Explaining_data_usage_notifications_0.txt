QuickConnect Customer Service - Data Usage Notifications




Supporting Customers with Data Usage Notifications
What are Data Usage Notifications?
Data usage notifications are alerts sent to customers by QuickConnect to inform them about their current data usage levels. These notifications are useful for customers to manage their data consumption and avoid unexpected charges.
Types of Data Usage Notifications

Threshold Notifications: Alerts when a customer has used 50%, 75%, 90%, and 100% of their data allowance.
Overage Notifications: Alerts when a customer exceeds their data allowance and incurs additional charges.

Common Customer Questions



Question
Possible Responses




Why did I receive a data usage notification?
Customers receive data usage notifications when they reach certain thresholds of their data allowance (50%, 75%, 90%, 100%) to help them manage their usage and avoid extra charges.


Can I turn off data usage notifications?
Yes, customers can opt out of receiving data usage notifications via their QuickConnect account settings. However, we recommend keeping them enabled to avoid unexpected charges.


How can I check my data usage?
Customers can check their data usage through the QuickConnect mobile app or by logging into their account on the QuickConnect website.


What should I do if I receive an overage notification?
If a customer receives an overage notification, they should consider reducing data usage or upgrading their plan to avoid additional charges in the future.
