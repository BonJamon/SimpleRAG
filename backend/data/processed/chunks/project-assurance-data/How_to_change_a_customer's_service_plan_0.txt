How to Change a Customer's Service Plan
Changing a customer's service plan at QuickConnect involves several steps to ensure the new plan meets the customer's needs and is correctly implemented in our system. Follow the steps outlined below:
Steps to Change a Customer's Service Plan


Identify Customer Details:

Verify the customer’s account information (name, address, account number).
Confirm the current service plan and usage details.



Discuss New Plan Options:

Understand the customer's needs and recommend suitable plans.
Discuss the features, pricing, and benefits of the new plans.



Obtain Customer Consent:

Ensure the customer agrees to the terms and conditions of the new plan.
Record customer consent either through a written agreement or a recorded verbal agreement.



Update the Plan in the System:

Log in to the QuickConnect system.
Navigate to the customer’s account page.
Select the new service plan and apply changes.
Verify that the new plan details are updated correctly.



Confirm Changes with the Customer:

Inform the customer that the plan change is complete.
Explain any immediate changes or interruptions in service (if applicable).



Provide Summary:

Send a summary of the new plan details to the customer’s email.
Include information on the next billing cycle and any prorated charges.
