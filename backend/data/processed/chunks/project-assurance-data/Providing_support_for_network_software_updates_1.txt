Device Type
Update Steps


Router


Log in to the router's admin panel.
Navigate to the 'Firmware Update' section.
Check for available updates and click 'Download'.
Once the download is complete, click 'Install'.
Wait for the router to restart and complete the update.




Modem


Access the modem settings through a connected device or its web interface.
Go to the 'Software Update' section.
Select 'Check for Updates'.
If an update is available, follow the prompts to download and install it.
Allow the modem to restart and finalize the update process.




Mobile App


Open the app and log in to your account.
Go to 'Settings' or 'About' section.
Select 'Check for App Update'.
If an update is available, follow the on-screen instructions to update.
Restart the app to complete the update.
