Common Issues and Solutions



Issue
Solution




Customer cannot receive recovery email
Guide the customer to check their spam/junk folder. Ensure that the recovery email was sent to the correct address.


Incorrect security answers
Verify identity through alternative questions or provide options to reset security questions.


Account locked due to multiple failed login attempts
Confirm identity and manually unlock the account or provide steps to unlock via the website.


Customer forgot recovery email or phone number
Verify identity through alternative methods and update recovery information in the system.
