Device Connectivity

Ensure the device is properly connected to the Wi-Fi or via Ethernet cable.
Turn off and on the device's Wi-Fi or replug the Ethernet cable.
Restart the device.



Network Settings

Check the network settings on the device to ensure it is connected to the correct network.
Forget the network and reconnect by entering the Wi-Fi password again.



Internet Speed Test

Use an online speed test tool to measure the current internet speed.
Compare the results with the expected speed for the customer's plan.
If speeds are significantly lower, proceed with further diagnostics or escalate.



VPN Troubleshooting

Ensure VPN software is up to date.
Check VPN settings and ensure proper configuration.
Consult the company's IT department for specific VPN issues.



Advanced Troubleshooting and Escalation
If the above steps do not resolve the issue, it may require advanced troubleshooting or escalation to technical support:

Document the steps taken and the results.
Collect relevant customer information such as:

            
Account number
Router/Modem make and model
