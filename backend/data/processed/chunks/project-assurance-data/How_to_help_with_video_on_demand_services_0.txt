QuickConnect Customer Support Guide: Video on Demand Services



QuickConnect Customer Support Guide
How to Help with Video on Demand Services
This guide will help you assist customers with their Video on Demand (VoD) services issues. Follow the steps and utilize the resources provided to ensure a seamless customer experience.


Common Issues
Solutions


Buffering or Slow Streaming


Check the customer’s internet speed. Recommend a minimum of 25 Mbps for HD streaming.
Advise the customer to restart their modem/router.
Suggest using a wired connection instead of Wi-Fi for better stability.




Unable to Access VoD Content


Ensure the customer is subscribed to the VoD service.
Verify that the customer's account is active and in good standing.
Check for any service outages in the customer's area.




Error Messages


Note the exact error message displayed.
Search our internal knowledge base for specific error codes.
If unresolved, escalate the issue to Level 2 support.




Login Issues


Verify the customer's username and password.
Guide the customer through the password reset process if needed.
Ensure the customer’s device and app are updated to the latest version.




Basic Troubleshooting Steps

Verify the customer's personal and account details.
Check the customer's internet connection and speed.
Ensure the device and application software are up to date.
Restart the device and modem/router.
Clear the application cache or reinstall the application.
