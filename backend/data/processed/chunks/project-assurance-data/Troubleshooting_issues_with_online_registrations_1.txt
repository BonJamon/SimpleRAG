Step-by-Step Troubleshooting Guide

Verify Customer Details:

                    
Confirm the customer's full name, email address, and contact number.
Check for any spelling errors or incorrect information.


Technical Checks:

                    
Ensure the customer's device meets the minimum system requirements.
Advise customers to try an alternative browser or clear their browser cache.


Account Verification:

                    
Guide the customer through the email verification process step-by-step.
If needed, escalate the issue to the account management team for further verification.



Contacting Technical Support
If the customer continues to experience issues after following the troubleshooting steps, please gather the necessary details and escalate the issue to our technical support team.
Information to Collect:

Customer's Name
Email Address
Contact Number
Detailed description of the issue
Screenshots of any error messages (if available)

Contact Technical Support via:

Internal Support Portal: http://support.quickconnect.com
