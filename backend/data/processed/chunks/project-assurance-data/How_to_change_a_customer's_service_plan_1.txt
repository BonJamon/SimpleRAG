Will the customer be charged a fee for changing their service plan?
No, QuickConnect does not charge a fee for service plan changes. However, be sure to discuss any prorated charges that may apply.


Can a customer change their plan more than once in a billing cycle?
Yes, customers can change their plan as needed, but it's important to inform them of potential prorated charges and the impact on their billing.


What happens if the customer wants to revert to their old plan?
Customers can revert to their previous plan, subject to availability and any current promotions or changes in pricing.
