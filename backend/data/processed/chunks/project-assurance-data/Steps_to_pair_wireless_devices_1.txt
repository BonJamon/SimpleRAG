Verify the Connection:

Some devices may require a passkey or code. Enter the code if prompted. Once the pairing is successful, a confirmation message should appear.



Test the Connection:

To ensure the devices are properly paired, test the functionality by playing audio, making a call, or accessing the wireless features of the paired device.



Troubleshooting Tips



Issue
Solution




Device not found
Ensure the device is in pairing mode and within range. Restart both devices and try again.


Incorrect passkey
Double-check the passkey and ensure you enter it correctly. Some devices have a default passkey like "0000" or "1234".


Connection drops
Move the devices closer together, and ensure there are no obstacles or other devices causing interference. Restart the devices and try pairing again.
