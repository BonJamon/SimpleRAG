Plan Restrictions
Some plans may have restrictions such as limited minutes, specific country access, or additional charges for certain services.


Coverage
Ensure that the plan covers the countries the customer intends to call frequently. Coverage can vary between plans.


Billing Cycle
Inform the customer about when the billing cycle starts and ends, and how international calls are billed.



Steps to Modify or Manage International Calling Plans

Access the Customer Account: Log into the customer account through the customer management portal.
Review Current Plan: Check the customer’s current plan details, including usage and billing history.
Discuss Needs: Ask the customer about their international calling habits and needs to recommend the best plan.
Plan Options: Present various plan options that fit the customer's needs, including detailed rate and restriction information.
Update Plan: Assist the customer in updating their plan through the portal, ensuring all terms are explained clearly.
Confirm Changes: Confirm the plan changes in the system and inform the customer about the effective date and any pro-rated charges.
Follow-Up: Schedule a follow-up call or message to ensure the customer is satisfied with the new plan.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How can a customer check their international call usage?
A: Customers can check their usage through the QuickConnect customer portal under the “Usage” tab or contact customer service for detailed reports.
