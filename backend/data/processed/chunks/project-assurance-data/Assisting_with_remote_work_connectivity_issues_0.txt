Assisting with Remote Work Connectivity Issues
Remote work relies heavily on stable and fast internet connectivity. QuickConnect aims to provide exceptional service and support for customers facing connectivity issues. This document outlines steps and troubleshooting methods to assist customers efficiently.
Common Connectivity Issues



Issue
Description




Slow Internet Speed
Internet speed is significantly slower than expected or required for work tasks.


Frequent Disconnections
Internet connection drops frequently, disrupting online activities such as video calls.


No Connectivity
Unable to connect to the internet at all.


VPN Issues
Difficulty connecting or staying connected to a VPN (Virtual Private Network).



Troubleshooting Steps


Check Router/Modem

Ensure the router/modem is plugged in and powered on.
Restart the router/modem by unplugging it for 30 seconds, then plugging it back in.
Check if the lights on the router/modem indicate a proper connection.



Device Connectivity

Ensure the device is properly connected to the Wi-Fi or via Ethernet cable.
Turn off and on the device's Wi-Fi or replug the Ethernet cable.
Restart the device.



Network Settings

Check the network settings on the device to ensure it is connected to the correct network.
Forget the network and reconnect by entering the Wi-Fi password again.
