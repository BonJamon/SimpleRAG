Troubleshooting Tips

Verify the customer's current speed by having them perform a speed test.
Check for any network congestion or usage spikes that may affect speed.
Ensure the customer's router and modem are properly configured and up to date.
Advise customers on ways to optimize their network, such as reducing the number of connected devices.
