5. Shipping Label and Instructions
Guide the customer on printing the shipping label. Provide the following options and instructions:

Download and print the label from the QuickConnect website.
If the customer does not have a printer, inform them they'll receive a label via mail within 3-5 business days.
Affix the label securely to the package, covering any old labels.

6. Drop-off Locations
Advise the customer on where to drop off the package:

Authorized QuickConnect return locations.
Courier service drop-off points (e.g., UPS, FedEx).
Arrange a pick-up if the customer is unable to visit a drop-off location.

7. Confirmation and Follow-up
Encourage the customer to keep the receipt as proof of return. Inform them that they will receive a confirmation email once the return is processed. The processing time is usually within 7-10 business days.
