Bill Structure

Section
Description




Account Summary
This section provides a summary of the total amount due, previous balance, payments received, and the current charges.


Service Summary
A detailed summary of the services provided during the billing period, including internet, phone, and TV services.


Usage Details
Breakdown of data, call, and message usage. Includes overage charges if applicable.

A detailed itemization of taxes, regulatory fees, and other governmental charges.


Promotions and Discounts
Any promotions or discounts that have been applied to the account for the billing period.

Any additional information or messages from QuickConnect.



Common Customer Questions
Here is a list of common questions customers might have about their bill and how to address them:


Why is my bill higher than last month?
Check the Usage Details section for any overage charges or additional services that may have been used. Also, review the Promotions and Discounts section to see if any promotional credits have ended.


What are these additional fees?
Direct the customer to the Taxes and Fees section where they will find a detailed breakdown of all additional charges.
