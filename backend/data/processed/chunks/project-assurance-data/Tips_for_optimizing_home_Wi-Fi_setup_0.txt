Tips for Optimizing Home Wi-Fi Setup
For an optimal home Wi-Fi experience, it is important to follow certain guidelines and make a few adjustments to ensure that your network is running smoothly and efficiently. Below are some essential tips for optimizing your home Wi-Fi setup.
Positioning the Router

Central Location: Place the router in a central location to provide equal coverage throughout your home.
Elevated Position: Position the router on a high shelf or mount it on the wall to improve signal strength and reduce interference.
Away from Obstructions: Keep the router away from thick walls, metal objects, and electronic devices to minimize interference.
