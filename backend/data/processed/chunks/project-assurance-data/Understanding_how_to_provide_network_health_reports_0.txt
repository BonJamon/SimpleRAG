Understanding How to Provide Network Health Reports
Introduction
Network Health Reports offer crucial insights into the performance and stability of a customer's telecommunications network. These reports help detect issues early, ensuring optimal network service performance. This guide will help you understand how to provide these reports effectively.
Steps to Generate a Network Health Report

Access the Customerâ€™s Account: Login to the QuickConnect admin dashboard and navigate to the customer's account details.
Navigate to the Network Health Section: Click on the 'Network Health' tab to access the network performance analytics.
Select the Time Frame: Choose the time interval for which the report needs to be generated (e.g., last 24 hours, last week, last month).
Generate the Report: Click on the 'Generate Report' button. This may take a few moments depending on the data volume.
Download or Email the Report: Once generated, the report can be downloaded as a PDF or sent directly to the customer's email.
