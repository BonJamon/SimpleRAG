macOS

Open System Preferences and click on Screen Time.
Enable Screen Time and select your child's account from the options.
Set the desired restrictions under App Limits, Downtime, and Content & Privacy settings.
Click OK to save the changes.

Setting Up Parental Controls on Streaming Services
Netflix

Log in to your Netflix account and navigate to Account Settings.
Select Parental Controls.
Enter your Netflix password and create a 4-digit PIN.
Select the maturity level for the content and chosen profiles.
Click Save to apply the parental controls.
