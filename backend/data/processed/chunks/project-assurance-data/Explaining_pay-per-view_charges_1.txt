Can PPV charges be refunded?
Refunds for PPV charges are typically not provided unless there was a service error, such as the content not being available or unwatchable due to technical issues. Review the specifics of each request to determine eligibility.


How can I prevent accidental PPV purchases?
Customers can set parental controls or pin codes on their account to restrict unauthorized PPV orders. Guide them through the process if they are interested.
