3. Configuring Switches

Enable VLANs (Virtual Local Area Networks) to segment network traffic and improve security and performance.
Configure link aggregation to combine multiple network connections for increased bandwidth and redundancy.
Utilize Quality of Service (QoS) to prioritize critical data traffic.

4. Configuring Access Points (APs)

Place APs strategically to ensure optimal coverage and minimal overlap.
Configure APs to operate on different channels to reduce interference.
Consider implementing Wi-Fi 6 for better performance, especially in high-density environments.

5. Monitoring and Maintenance
Regularly monitor network performance and maintain devices to ensure optimal performance:

View usage statistics and identify bottlenecks through network monitoring tools.
Restart network devices periodically to clear any temporary issues.
Schedule regular firmware updates to ensure device stability and security.
