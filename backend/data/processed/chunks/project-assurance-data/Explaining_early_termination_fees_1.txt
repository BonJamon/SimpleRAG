How to Explain Early Termination Fees to Customers
When discussing early termination fees with customers, it is important to:

Be transparent about the terms and conditions of their contract.
Provide a clear breakdown of how the fee is calculated based on their specific contract.
Gently remind the customer that these fees help cover the costs of the services and equipment provided at a discounted rate.
Offer alternatives, such as changing service plans, that may not incur these fees.


Note: Always review the customer's account details and contract before providing specific fee amounts to ensure accuracy.
