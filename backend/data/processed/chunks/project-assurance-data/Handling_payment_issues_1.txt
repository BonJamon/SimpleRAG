FAQs for Payment Issues

Q: What should I do if my payment fails?

                A: Verify the payment method, ensure sufficient funds, and try again. If the issue persists, contact customer support.

            
Q: How can I request a refund?

                A: Submit a refund request through your account portal or contact customer support for help.
