Frequently Asked Questions

Can I remove a family member from the group?
Yes, as the Family Organizer, you can remove members. Go to 'Family Sharing,' select the member you want to remove, and click on 'Remove from Family Group.'


Is there a limit to the number of family members?
You can add up to 6 members (including yourself) to your Family Group.


Will family members have access to my purchase history?
No, family members will not have access to your individual purchase history; they will only share access to purchased items you're allowed to share.
