Do I need a new device for 5G?
Yes, a 5G-enabled device is required to access 5G network services. Check with QuickConnect for the latest 5G-compatible devices.


Will my existing plan support 5G?
Most current plans support 5G. However, some older plans may need an upgrade. Please review the customer’s plan details or consult our plan guide.


Is 5G safe?
Yes, 5G technology is safe and meets all international health and safety guidelines. It uses radio frequencies that are similar to existing cellular networks.


Customer Tips

Here are a few tips to help customers get the best experience with 5G:

Ensure they have a 5G-compatible device.
Verify their location is within our 5G coverage area.
Advise them to update their device’s software to the latest version.
