Free Local Resources
Customers can also take advantage of free local resources to improve their digital literacy:

Public Libraries: Many libraries offer free computer classes and internet access.
Community Centers: Look for workshops and courses at community centers in your area.
Non-Profit Organizations: Some non-profits provide digital literacy programs for different age groups.

Tips for Improving Digital Literacy

Start by learning the basics of computer use, such as how to operate a mouse and keyboard, and navigate the operating system.
Practice safe internet usage, including recognizing phishing scams, creating strong passwords, and understanding privacy settings.
Explore and familiarize yourself with commonly used software and applications like word processors, spreadsheets, and email clients.
Stay up-to-date with the latest technology trends and updates by subscribing to tech newsletters or following tech blogs.
