QuickConnect Customer Service Guide
How to Reset Customer's Voicemail Password

Note: Ensure you have the customer's account details and proper verification before proceeding with these steps.

Steps to Reset Voicemail Password:

Verify Customer Identity: Confirm the customer's identity by asking for their account number, billing address, and a security question answer.
Access the Account: Log in to the QuickConnect account management system using your employee credentials.
Locate Voicemail Settings:

Go to the customer's profile.
Click on the "Services" tab.
Select "Voicemail Settings."


Reset Voicemail Password:

Click on "Reset Voicemail Password."
Enter a temporary password or allow the system to generate one automatically.
Inform the customer of the temporary password.
Advise the customer to change the temporary password as soon as possible.



Important Notes:

The temporary password is usually 1234 or another pre-set default. Confirm this with the system instructions.
If the customer encounters any issues, advise them to call customer support or use the online help portal.

FAQs:


Question
Answer


What if the customer forgot their account number?
Ask for other identifying information (e.g., billing address, last payment date) to locate their account.


What temporary password should be set?
Use the default system-generated password or "1234" unless otherwise specified.
