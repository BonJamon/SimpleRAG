Step-by-Step Installation Guide
1. Understanding the Need for Backup Battery
A backup battery ensures that your device continues to operate during power outages. It maintains your internet connectivity and phone services, providing critical support during emergencies.
2. Identifying Your Device
Different devices have different backup battery requirements. Refer to the table below to identify if your device needs a backup battery:



Device Model
Requires Backup Battery




QuickConnect Router 3000
Yes


QuickConnect Modem X50
No


QuickConnect Home Hub
Yes



3. Installation Steps

Ensure your device is powered off.
Locate the battery compartment (refer to your device manual if needed).
Insert the backup battery into the compartment, ensuring correct polarity (+/-).
Secure the battery cover back in place.
Power on your device and check for any battery status indicators.

Troubleshooting Tips
Common Issues and Solutions



Issue
Solution




Device does not power on with battery
Check the battery installation for correct polarity and secure connections.


Battery status indicator shows low charge
Ensure the battery is fully charged by connecting the device to a power source for at least 4 hours.


Battery compartment cover does not close properly
Make sure the battery is positioned correctly and not obstructing the cover.
