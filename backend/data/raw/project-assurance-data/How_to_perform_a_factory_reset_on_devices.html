Factory Reset Guide

Performing a factory reset on your QuickConnect device can help resolve many issues by restoring the device to its original settings. Follow the guide below to perform a factory reset on different QuickConnect devices.

Device Type Instructions
Router
  1. Ensure the router is powered on.
  2. Locate the reset button on the router. It is usually a small, recessed button.
  3. Use a paperclip or a similar object to press and hold the reset button for 10-15 seconds.
  4. Release the reset button and wait for the router to reboot.
  5. The router is now reset to factory settings. Reconfigure the router if necessary.
Modem
  1. Ensure the modem is powered on.
  2. Locate the reset button or reset hole on the modem.
  3. Press and hold the reset button for at least 10 seconds using a pointed object.
  4. Release the button and wait for the modem to restart.
  5. Once the lights on the modem stabilize, the modem is reset to factory settings.
Smartphone
  1. Navigate to the Settings menu on your smartphone.
  2. Select "Backup & reset" or "System", then choose "Factory data reset".
  3. You may be prompted to enter your passcode or unlock pattern.
  4. Confirm by selecting "Reset" or "Erase everything".
  5. Wait for the phone to reset and restart. This may take several minutes.
Tablet
  1. Open the Settings menu on your tablet.
  2. Choose "System" and then "Reset options".
  3. Select "Erase all data (factory reset)".
  4. Confirm your selection and follow the on-screen prompts to initiate the reset.
  5. Allow the tablet to reset and restart. This process can take a few minutes.

Important Notes

  • Backing up your data is recommended before performing a factory reset, as this process will erase all personal data and settings.
  • If you encounter any issues during the reset process, contact QuickConnect Customer Support for assistance.
  • After a factory reset, you may need to reconfigure your device settings and reconnect to your Wi-Fi network.
