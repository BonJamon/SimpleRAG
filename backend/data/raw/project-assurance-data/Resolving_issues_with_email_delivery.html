Resolving Issues with Email Delivery

Email delivery issues can be caused by a variety of factors. This guide will help customer service agents at QuickConnect resolve common problems related to email delivery.

Common Issues and Solutions

Issue Possible Causes Solutions
Emails Not Received
  • Incorrect email address
  • Email filter settings
  • Email service downtime
  • Confirm the recipient's email address
  • Check the spam/junk folder
  • Verify email server status
Emails Going to Spam
  • Spam filters
  • Email content
  • Recipient's email settings
  • Modify the email content
  • Ask the recipient to mark the email as 'Not Spam'
  • Review and update email sending practices
Emails Delayed
  • Network issues
  • Server overload
  • Email size
  • Check network connectivity
  • Contact email service provider
  • Reduce the size of attachments

Troubleshooting Steps

  • Verify Email Settings: Ensure that the email client settings are correct.
  • Check Connectivity: Confirm that the internet connection is stable and secure.
  • Review Email Queues: Look for emails stuck in the outbox or drafts.
  • Contact Support: If the issue persists, contact the email service provider for further assistance.

Note: Always make sure that customers are using the latest version of their email client and that it is properly configured to avoid any known bugs or issues.
