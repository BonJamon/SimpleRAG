QuickConnect Customer Service Guide

Assisting Customers with Account Recovery

Overview

Helping customers recover their accounts is a crucial part of providing excellent customer service. This guide outlines the steps and procedures QuickConnect customer service agents should follow to assist customers effectively.

Account Recovery Steps

  1. Verify Customer Identity:
    • Ask for account-specific information (e.g., last bill amount, registered email).
    • Check the validity of the provided information against the database.
  2. Password Reset:
    • Guide the customer to the "Forgot Password" page on the QuickConnect website.
    • Assist the customer in receiving a password reset email or SMS.
    • Follow up to ensure the customer successfully resets their password.
  3. Unlocked Account:
    • Check account status in the system to determine why the account is locked.
    • Provide instructions to the customer to unlock the account.
    • Confirm that the account is unlocked and accessible.
  4. Update Account Information:
    • Help the customer update their security questions and answers.
    • Assist with updating contact information (email, phone number) for account recovery.
  5. Additional Support:
    • Escalate the issue to the technical support team if necessary.
    • Provide the customer with a case number for future follow-ups.

Common Issues and Solutions

Issue Solution
Customer cannot receive recovery email Guide the customer to check their spam/junk folder. Ensure that the recovery email was sent to the correct address.
Incorrect security answers Verify identity through alternative questions or provide options to reset security questions.
Account locked due to multiple failed login attempts Confirm identity and manually unlock the account or provide steps to unlock via the website.
Customer forgot recovery email or phone number Verify identity through alternative methods and update recovery information in the system.
