Troubleshooting Online Registration Issues

Overview

This document provides customer service agents with a comprehensive guide to troubleshooting common issues customers might encounter during online registration for the QuickConnect services.

Common Issues and Solutions

Issue Possible Cause Solution
Error Message: "Email already in use" The email address is already associated with an existing account. Advise the customer to use a different email address or to reset their password if they have previously registered.
Page Not Loading or Timing Out Poor internet connection or server issues. Ask the customer to check their internet connection and retry after some time. If the issue persists, escalate to the technical support team.
Verification Email Not Received Email filters might be blocking the verification email, or the email address was entered incorrectly. Suggest the customer to check their spam/junk folder, ensure the email address is correct, and resend the verification email.

Step-by-Step Troubleshooting Guide

  • Verify Customer Details:
    • Confirm the customer's full name, email address, and contact number.
    • Check for any spelling errors or incorrect information.
  • Technical Checks:
    • Ensure the customer's device meets the minimum system requirements.
    • Advise customers to try an alternative browser or clear their browser cache.
  • Account Verification:
    • Guide the customer through the email verification process step-by-step.
    • If needed, escalate the issue to the account management team for further verification.

Contacting Technical Support

If the customer continues to experience issues after following the troubleshooting steps, please gather the necessary details and escalate the issue to our technical support team.

Information to Collect:

  • Customer's Name
  • Email Address
  • Contact Number
  • Detailed description of the issue
  • Screenshots of any error messages (if available)
