Understanding International Calling Rates

Welcome to the QuickConnect customer service guide on understanding and explaining international calling rates. Here you will find detailed information to help customers understand these rates and assist them with any related inquiries.

What are International Calling Rates?

International calling rates are charges that apply when customers make phone calls to numbers outside their home country. These rates vary depending on the destination, the type of call (landline or mobile), and the customerâ€™s calling plan.

Key Factors Affecting International Calling Rates

  • Destination Country: Rates vary by the country being called.
  • Type of Call: Charges can differ if the call is to a landline or a mobile number.
  • Calling Plan: Customers may have plans that include discounted rates for international calls or inclusive minute packages.
  • Service Provider: Rates can vary between different telecommunications providers.

How to Check International Calling Rates

Customers can check international calling rates through the following ways:

  • Online Portal: Direct customers to the QuickConnect website to view current rates.
  • Customer Support: Encourage customers to call our support line for personalized assistance.
  • Email Inquiry: Customers can also send an email to receive detailed information about rates.
