Steps to Return Rented Equipment

Follow the steps below to guide your customers through returning their rented equipment to QuickConnect.

1. Identify the Equipment to be Returned

Make sure the customer has a list of all the equipment they need to return. Common items might include:

  • Routers
  • Modems
  • Set-top boxes or DVRs
  • Power adapters and cables

2. Check Return Eligibility

Ensure the equipment is eligible for return by checking the rental agreement and warranty terms. If uncertain, escalates to a supervisor.

3. Provide a Return Authorization Number (RAN)

Generate and provide a Return Authorization Number (RAN) for tracking purposes. The customer will need this number to proceed with the return.

4. Packaging Instructions

Instruct the customer on how to properly package the equipment to avoid any damage during the return shipment. Provide the following guidelines:

  • Use the original packaging if available.
  • Wrap items securely with bubble wrap or packing paper.
  • Avoid using newspaper or any material that may cause abrasion.
  • Place the equipment in a sturdy box and seal it with packing tape.

5. Shipping Label and Instructions

Guide the customer on printing the shipping label. Provide the following options and instructions:

  • Download and print the label from the QuickConnect website.
  • If the customer does not have a printer, inform them they'll receive a label via mail within 3-5 business days.
  • Affix the label securely to the package, covering any old labels.

6. Drop-off Locations

Advise the customer on where to drop off the package:

  • Authorized QuickConnect return locations.
  • Courier service drop-off points (e.g., UPS, FedEx).
  • Arrange a pick-up if the customer is unable to visit a drop-off location.

7. Confirmation and Follow-up

Encourage the customer to keep the receipt as proof of return. Inform them that they will receive a confirmation email once the return is processed. The processing time is usually within 7-10 business days.
