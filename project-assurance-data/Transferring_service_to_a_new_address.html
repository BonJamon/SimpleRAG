QuickConnect Customer Service Guide

Transferring Service to a New Address

Steps to Transfer Service
  1. Gather Necessary Information
    • Current Account Number
    • New Address Details
    • Preferred Installation Date
  2. Contact QuickConnect Customer Service
    • Call our support line at 1-800-123-4567
    • Or email support@quickconnect.com
    • Provide the gathered information
  3. Verification Process
    • Verify identity using account security questions
    • Confirm new address availability for QuickConnect services
  4. Schedule Installation
    • Choose an available installation date
    • Receive a confirmation email
  5. Prepare for Installation
    • Ensure someone is present during the scheduled installation time
    • Keep any required documents handy
Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
Question Answer
How much does the service transfer cost? The cost may vary depending on your location and service plan. Please contact customer support for accurate pricing.
How long does the transfer process take? The process generally takes between 3-7 business days depending on the availability of service in the new area and the preferred installation date.
Will there be any interruption to my service? There may be a brief interruption during the transfer, but we strive to minimize downtime. Your service at the old address will be active until the day of the transfer.
Can I keep my current phone number? If you are transferring within the same area code, it is generally possible to keep your current phone number. Please confirm with our customer service team.
