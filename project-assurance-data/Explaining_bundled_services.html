Understanding Bundled Services at QuickConnect

Bundled services are packages that combine multiple QuickConnect services at a discounted rate. This document will help customer service agents understand and explain these bundled services to customers effectively.

What are Bundled Services?

Bundled services offer customers a convenient and cost-effective way to access multiple telecommunications services, including:

  • Internet
  • Television
  • Phone
  • Mobile services

Benefits of Bundled Services

  • Cost Savings: Bundles often have lower monthly costs compared to purchasing services individually.
  • Convenience: Single billing and customer support for all services in the bundle.
  • Special Features: Additional perks such as premium channels, free installation, and more.
  • Customization: Options available to tailor bundles to specific customer needs.
