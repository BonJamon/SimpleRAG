QuickConnect Customer Service Guide

Explaining Monthly Usage Reports

Purpose: This document will help customer service agents understand how to explain monthly usage reports to QuickConnect customers.

What is a Monthly Usage Report?

A Monthly Usage Report is a detailed summary of a customer's telecommunications usage over a specific month. It includes data on calls, SMS, data usage, and any additional services the customer has utilized.

Key Components of the Report

Component Description
Account Summary Overview of the customer's account information, including billing cycle dates, plan details, and total charges.
Call Summary Breakdown of all calls made, received, and missed, including duration and cost per call.
Data Usage Detailed information on data consumption, distinguishing between cellular and Wi-Fi usage (if available).
SMS Summary Total number of SMS messages sent and received, including any applicable charges.
Additional Services Charges and usage related to any additional services, such as international calls or roaming.

  • What should I do if I notice errors in my report? Advise the customer to contact QuickConnect customer support for a detailed review and potential rectification of any errors.

Best Practices for Explaining Reports

  1. Stay Calm and Polite: Always maintain a friendly demeanor, especially if the customer is confused or frustrated.
  2. Break Down Information: Simplify complex details into easier-to-understand segments.
  3. Use Visual Aids: Guide the customer on where to look in their online report or provide sample images if necessary.
  4. Encourage Questions: Prompt the customer to ask questions to ensure they've fully understood their report.
  5. Verify Understanding: Recap key points to confirm the customer comprehends the information shared.
