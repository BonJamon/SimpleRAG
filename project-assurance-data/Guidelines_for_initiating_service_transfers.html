Guidelines for Initiating Service Transfers

Note: Service transfers are the process by which a current QuickConnect service plan is moved from one location to another or from one customer to another. These should be handled with care to ensure seamless continuity of service.

Types of Service Transfers

  • Residential Transfer: Moving services from one residential address to another.
  • Business Transfer: Transferring services from one business address to another.
  • Customer Transfer: Changing ownership of the service from one customer to another.

Steps for Initiating a Service Transfer

  1. Customer Verification: Verify the identity of the customer requesting the transfer. Ensure they have the necessary permissions and that their account is in good standing.
  2. Collect Transfer Details: Gather all relevant information including:
    • Current and new service addresses
    • Preferred transfer date
    • Contact information of new customers (if applicable)
  3. Check for Service Availability: Confirm that QuickConnect services are available at the new location.
  4. Schedule the Transfer: Coordinate a date and time for the service transfer, keeping in mind the customerâ€™s preferred date.
  5. Service Confirmation: Inform the customer about the transfer details and confirm the service transfer appointment.
  6. Installation and Setup: Ensure that all technical setups and installations are completed on the scheduled date.
  7. Follow-Up: Contact the customer post-transfer to ensure everything is working as expected and address any issues promptly.

Common Issues and Solutions

Issue Solution
Service Not Available at New Location Check for alternative solutions or offer the customer different service options available in the area.
Transfer Delays Communicate delays to the customer as soon as possible and provide a new expected transfer date.
Technical Glitches Post-Transfer Dispatch a technician for on-site assistance and provide 24/7 customer support through the resolution process.
