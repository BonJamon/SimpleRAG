Upgrading a Customer's Internet Plan

This document provides detailed steps to assist customers in upgrading their internet plan efficiently and effectively.

Steps to Follow

  1. Log in to the customer’s account using the CRM system.
  2. Verify customer information to ensure account security.
  3. Navigate to the "Internet Services" section of the customer account.
  4. Review the current internet plan details with the customer.
  5. Discuss and recommend available upgrade options based on customer needs.
  6. Process the upgrade request through the CRM system.
  7. Confirm the changes made with the customer and provide a summary of the new plan details.
  8. Inform the customer about any potential service interruptions during the upgrade process.

Important Notes

  • Ensure all upgrade requests are made with customer consent.
  • Clearly explain the benefits and any additional charges related to the upgrade.
  • Verify if the customer’s equipment is compatible with the new plan.
  • Provide information on any promotions or discounts that may be available.
